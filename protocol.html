Semantic Protocol

语义协议 · A protocol for encoding, transmitting and reconstructing meaning across languages and systems.

Protocol is no longer just about data.

It is about meaning.

Unilanguage extends protocol from data transmission to meaning interoperability. It treats language as a system of semantic operations, symbols, relations and reconstruction rules.

Meaning
①

Encode

语义编码

Convert words, symbols and contexts into structured semantic units.

②

Transmit

语义传输

Carry meaning across languages, machines, people and cultures.

③

Reconstruct

语义重构

Rebuild meaning in another language, system or cognitive environment.

Protocol Layers · 协议层

1. Sound Layer 声音层 · phonetic form, rhythm, tone and sound direction.
2. Symbol Layer 符号层 · letters, characters, signs and writing systems.
3. Meaning Layer 语义层 · concepts, semantic fields and core meanings.
4. Mapping Layer 映射层 · correspondences between English, Chinese, French and future languages.
5. Reconstruction Layer 重构层 · rebuild expression in another linguistic or intelligent system.
