Unilanguage 是什么？

What is Unilanguage?

Unilanguage 是一个基于语义的语言统一协议系统，旨在打破自然语言的界限，建立一个可互通、可计算、可无限扩展的全新语言生态。


Unilanguage is a semantic-based language unification protocol system designed to build a new linguistic ecosystem.
